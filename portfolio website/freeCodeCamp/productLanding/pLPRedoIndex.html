About Us

Our Story

Cream de la Cream Puffs is a local bakery specializing in puff pastries of all kinds. We source all of our ingredients locally, working with farms across the tennessee valley to ensure the freshest ingreedients.

Menu

Our menu changes weekly to ensure the freshness of our locally sourced ingredients. We believe the future is most secure when we support local farms and orchards in the Tennesee valley.
