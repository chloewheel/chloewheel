Teriyaki Salmon and Veggies

sweet and savory salmon dish

Ingredients

2 tbs soy-sauce

2 tbs honey

1 tbs corn starch

1/4 cup zucchini

1/4 cup corn

1/4 cup broccoli

3 heads bok choi

1 fillet fresh salmon

Cooking instructions

Place salmon in non-stick pan on medium heat, cook until tan

In a seperate bowl, mix together soy sauce, honey and corn starch, set aside.

add veggies to pan with salmon. let simmer for 5 minutes.

pour sauce over fish and veggies, turn up to high heat, searing salmon on both sides
